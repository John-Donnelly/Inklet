# Privacy Policy — Inklet

**Last updated:** 2026-07-05
**Developer:** JAD Apps (John Donnelly)

---

## Overview

Inklet is a lightweight, local text editor for Windows. This policy describes what information the application accesses and how it is handled.

---

## Data Collection

**Inklet collects no personal data.**

The application does not collect, store, transmit, or share any information about you or your device.

---

## File Access

Inklet accesses files on your device **only at your explicit direction** — for example, when you open, save, or print a file. Files are read and written locally; their contents are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any third party, including the developer.

Local app settings (font, zoom level, window size, and session state) are stored on your device using the Windows application data store (`ApplicationData.Current.LocalSettings`). This data never leaves your device.

---

## Network Access

Inklet makes **no network connections** of any kind. There is no telemetry, analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or update checking.

---

## Third-Party Services

Inklet does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect data.

---

## Children's Privacy

Because Inklet collects no data, it presents no privacy risk to users of any age.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future release, the updated version will be committed to this repository and the **Last updated** date above will be revised.
